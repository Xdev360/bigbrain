Core functionalities to design

  1. Home balance view that prioritises readable amounts over network noise.
  2. Send and receive flows with confirmation states that reduce costly mistakes.
  3. Transaction history that explains what happened in plain language.
  4. Asset list and detail screens that stay scannable on small screens.
  5. Onboarding and security prompts that teach just enough, then get out of the way.
