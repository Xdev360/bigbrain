A food ordering app built for a campus town — menu browsing, cart and checkout designed for slow connections and first-time users who had never ordered food online before.

Operating range

User research / User experience design / Wireframing / Prototyping / UI design / Usability testing

Core functionalities to design

  1. Browse campus vendors with clear categories and load-friendly image treatment for weak networks.
  2. Menu exploration with modifiers, availability states and simple add-to-cart feedback.
  3. Cart and checkout reduced to the fewest steps possible for first-time users.
  4. Order status and history that keep people informed without technical jargon.
  5. Vendor and dish favourites for quick re-orders during peak campus hours.
